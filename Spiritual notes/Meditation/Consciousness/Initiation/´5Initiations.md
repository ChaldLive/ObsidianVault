# **Bailey ↔ Patanjali: Detailed Mapping of the First Five Initiations**

## **1️⃣ The Birth**

**Bailey:** _The Birth of the Christ in the Heart_  
**Patanjali:** _Śraddhā → Vīrya → Smṛti → Samādhi‑bhāvanā_ (I.20)

**Essence:**

- The first stable orientation toward the higher Self.
    
- A shift from personality-driven life to soul-awareness.
    
- Patanjali describes the same threshold: faith, energy, memory, and aspiration leading toward samādhi.
    

**Mechanism:**

- A new center of gravity forms in consciousness.
    
- The emotional nature begins to respond to buddhi.
    
- The disciple becomes inwardly directed.
    

---

## **2️⃣ The Baptism**

**Bailey:** _Purification of the emotional body_  
**Patanjali:** _Śauca, Santoṣa, Tapas_ (II.32) + _Pratyāhāra_ (II.54)

**Essence:**

- The astral/emotional field becomes transparent.
    
- Purification, discipline, and withdrawal from emotional reactivity.
    
- Patanjali’s niyamas describe the same process of inner cleansing.
    

**Mechanism:**

- Emotional turbulence is replaced by clarity.
    
- Desire loses its grip.
    
- The disciple becomes a stable reflector of soul light.
    

---

## **3️⃣ The Transfiguration**

**Bailey:** _Fusion of soul and personality_  
**Patanjali:** _Dharana → Dhyāna → Samādhi_ (III.1–III.3)

**Essence:**

- The mind becomes a unified, lighted instrument.
    
- The personality stands as a single coordinated field.
    
- Patanjali’s _samyama_ describes the same fusion of attention, flow, and identity.
    

**Mechanism:**

- Illumination.
    
- The soul’s radiance dominates the personality.
    
- The disciple becomes a conscious transmitter of light.
    

---

## **4️⃣ The Renunciation**

**Bailey:** _The Crucifixion; relinquishment of the personal self_  
**Patanjali:** _Paravairāgya_ (I.16)

**Essence:**

- The death of the personal center.
    
- Dispassion toward even the highest spiritual states.
    
- Patanjali’s supreme dispassion describes the same release of subtle identity threads.
    

**Mechanism:**

- Identity shifts from soul to spirit.
    
- The causal body begins to lose its hold.
    
- Purpose replaces aspiration.
    

---

## **5️⃣ The Revelation**

**Bailey:** _Revelation of the monad; spiritual will_  
**Patanjali:** _Dharma‑megha Samādhi_ (IV.29–IV.31)

**Essence:**

- Contact with the monadic will.
    
- The “raincloud of knowable things.”
    
- Karma is burned; the seeds of ignorance are destroyed.
    

**Mechanism:**

- The disciple becomes an agent of purpose.
    
- Will-as-being replaces will-as-effort.
    
- The higher triad becomes the operative centre.
