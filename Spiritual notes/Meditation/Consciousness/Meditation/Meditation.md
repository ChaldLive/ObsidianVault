# Five stages of meditation

---

- **_Concentration_** This is the act of concentrating the mind, learning to focus and so use it.

- **_Meditation_** The prolonged focussing of the attention in any direction and the steaduy holding of the mind any desired idea.

- **_Contemplation_** An activity of the soul, detached from the mind, which is held in a state of quiesence

- **_Illumination_** This is the result of the three preceding processes, and involves the carrying down into the brain consciousness of the
  knowledge achieved.

- _**Inspiration**_ The result of illumination, as it demonstrates in the life of service.
